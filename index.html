About Khalaba Ward Farmers' SACCO

We are a community-focused financial cooperative committed to empowering farmers through affordable financial solutions, training, and agricultural support. Our goal is to transform lives by fostering sustainable growth for farmers in our region.

How to Borrow

Access affordable loans tailored for your farming needs. Here's how you can borrow:

  • Become a registered member of our SACCO.
  • Submit a loan application with required documentation.
  • Undergo a credit assessment and approval process.
  • Receive your loan and start your project!

We offer flexible repayment plans and low-interest rates for all our loan products.

Become a Member

Enjoy exclusive benefits by becoming a member:

  • Fill out our membership form (available online or at our offices).
  • Provide identification documents (ID, KRA PIN, etc.).
  • Pay a small membership fee to activate your account.
  • Start enjoying our financial services and agricultural support programs.

Membership is open to all farmers in Khalaba Ward and beyond.

Save With Us

Secure your financial future by saving with us. Here's what we offer:

  • Regular savings accounts with competitive interest rates.
  • Fixed deposit accounts for higher returns.
  • Special savings plans for specific goals, like education or emergencies.

Your savings are safe and earn you consistent interest, helping you achieve your goals faster.

Other Services

In addition to saving and borrowing, we provide:

  • Mobile banking for easy transactions and account access.
  • Training and workshops on best agricultural practices.
  • Market linkage to connect farmers with buyers.
  • Farm inputs financing to ensure a successful farming season.
